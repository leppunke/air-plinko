# M18 CYCLONE PLINKO

## The Pitch

A massive plinko board where players **WIELD actual Milwaukee M18 blowers** as their controllers. Drop a ball from the top, then BLAST it with 120 MPH of M18 air power through side-mounted air ports to steer it toward high-scoring zones. The Milwaukee tools aren't just building the game — they ARE the game.

No outlet. No extension cord. Pure battery-powered chaos.

## Concept

Two players face a 4ft × 6ft vertical plinko board enclosed in clear acrylic. Balls drop from the top and cascade through a field of pegs. Along each side of the board, **three air ports** at different heights let players jam in their M18 blower nozzle and blast air across the field to deflect balls toward scoring pockets.

Each player is tethered to a **Milwaukee M18 FUEL Compact Blower** on a bungee leash. They physically move between air ports, choosing when and where to blast. The blower trigger is live — you squeeze, you blast.

When two players blast from opposite sides at the same time? **CYCLONE.** Balls go absolutely haywire.

## Gameplay

### Setup
- Each player gets 5 colored whiffle balls (Red vs Black — Milwaukee colors)
- Players stand on opposite sides of the board
- Each side has 3 air ports at LOW / MID / HIGH positions

### Turn Flow
1. **DROP** — Player places ball in one of 3 drop slots at the top of the board
2. **CHAOS** — Ball cascades through the peg field, bouncing unpredictably
3. **BLAST** — Player jams their M18 blower nozzle into a side air port and pulls the trigger, sending a 120 MPH air blast across the field
4. **STEER** — Air deflects the ball mid-fall toward (or away from) scoring zones
5. **SCORE** — Ball lands in a scoring pocket at the bottom, or falls into the gutter

### Scoring Pockets (Bottom of Board)
| Zone | Points | Width |
|------|--------|-------|
| JACKPOT (dead center) | 100 | 3" |
| HIGH (inner) | 50 | 4" × 2 |
| MID (middle) | 25 | 5" × 2 |
| LOW (outer) | 10 | 6" × 2 |
| GUTTER (edges) | 0 | — |

### Winning
- After all 5 balls each, highest score wins
- **CYCLONE BONUS**: If both players blast simultaneously and a ball lands in JACKPOT, it's worth 200

## Safety

- Blowers tethered with bungee leashes — can't be aimed at people or walked away with
- Air ports are sized so the nozzle seats firmly — air only goes into the board
- Acrylic front panel keeps all balls contained
- Trigger pull required — blower only runs when player intends it
- All batteries secured in tool clips mounted to the booth frame
- Operator can kill power by removing batteries if needed
